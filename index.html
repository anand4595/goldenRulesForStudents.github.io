Golden rules for learning

Today a reader Tomorrow a leader

Learn by doing

To gain perfection in a skill practice is the key. As is involves involve thoughtful responses, decision-making and solving problems encourage active learning and also promote higher order thinking.

Learn in small bits

Our brains have a limited capacity to perceive and process information. Breaking your content into small learning snacks or microlearning, will enhance comprehension and retention of knowledge and skills.

Be active, not passive

Practice or apply what you read as often as you can. Successful learners acquire better skills by doing.

Start with why

What do you want to achieve? How will the new knowledge improve your life or career? What difference will it make to your future self? A bigger why gives you momentum.

Clear basis first

Once you understand the first principles of any topic, you can quickly master the details. Don’t rush to learn complex ideas until you build a firm foundation.
